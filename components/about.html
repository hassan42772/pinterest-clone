About Pinterest

Our mission is to bring everyone the inspiration to create a life they love

What is Pinterest?

Pinterest is a visual discovery engine for finding ideas like recipes, home and style inspiration, and more. With billions of Pins on Pinterest, you'll always find ideas to spark inspiration.

How it works

When you discover Pins you love, save them to boards to keep your ideas organized and easy to find. You can create boards for any project or category you want.

Our community

Millions of people use Pinterest to discover and do what they love. Join our community to find your inspiration and share your passions with others.
